Below we have our various services offered and a breakdown thereof

Sculptured Acrylic

Sculptured Acrylic




  • These sets are strong and durable nail extensions built on the natural nail

  • Uses nail forms as a base for the extension

  • Leaves a light feel on your nail as the acrylic will follow your nail’s natural growth pattern

  • creates a desired length and shape of your choice



  • Prices starting from R300
    •

    Acrylic Overlay

    Acrylic Overlay




  • Applied on top of the length of the natural nail, without forms, to reinforce brittle/weak nails

  • Acts as a protective layer while still keeping a natural look to the nail

  • Overlays are recommended to clients who’s nails tend to chip/break easily.



  • Price starting from R280
    •

    Mini Manicure/Pedicure




  • Soak, filing, cuticle work, buff and shine and/or paint.

  • Soak, exfoliation, light massage, filing cuticle work, buff and shine and/or paint.

  • Acts as a protective layer while still keeping a natural look to the nail



  • Price starting from R350

    Encapsulations

    Encapsulations




  • Acrylic powder is used to sculpt a desired length and shape

  • Encapsulations are a form of nail art

  • Nail art design makes use of glitter and other acrylic powders



  • Full set pricing starts at R510

    Ombré Sculptured Acrylic
